Do
✓ Use Deep Navy as the dominant brand color
✓ Use Iris Gold sparingly as a premium accent
✓ Pair Navy with Off White for maximum legibility
✓ Limit compositions to 3 palette colors maximum
Don't
✗ Place Gold on Sky Blue — insufficient contrast
✗ Use more than 3 brand colors in one layout
✗ Introduce any off-palette colors without approval
✗ Use Gold as a background color for large areas

Secondary Typeface — Body & UI Cabin
At Paul Optics, every lens is chosen with precision and care. Our consultants have guided patients to perfect vision for nearly four decades, combining advanced diagnostic technology with a deeply personal approach to eyecare.
Regular 400 Medium 500 SemiBold 600 Bold 700
Use 400 for body copy. 600–700 for UI labels, captions, and functional text. Never use italic Cabin for extended body copy.

✓ Write Like This
"Our optometrists bring precision and care to every consultation — because your vision deserves nothing less than the best."
✗ Not Like This
"Check out our awesome deals on specs! Get your eyes tested today — super easy, super quick, book now!"
Tone in Context
✓ Clinical content: precise, informative, reassuring
✓ Social media: warm, accessible, never casual
✓ Ads: confident, benefit-led, heritage-anchored
Always Avoid
✗ Slang, abbreviations, or overly casual language
✗ Superlatives without substance ("the best ever")
✗ Medical claims without clinical backing
